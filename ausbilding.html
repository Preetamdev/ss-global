Ausbildung & Study in Germany

Vocational Training Program

Vocational training in Germany is highly esteemed and is considered one of the best in the world. There are various sectors for vocational training, and completing a traineeship requires applicants to have finished a certain level of school education. After passing exams, trainees earn a nationally recognized qualification.

To pursue a vocational traineeship in Germany, applicants also need to provide a language certificate showing at least a B1 proficiency level according to the European framework.

A dual vocational traineeship involves both learning at a vocational school and gaining hands-on experience at a company. Trainees have a contract with the company responsible for their training and receive a salary throughout the course. Importantly, there are no fees to pay for this program.

Steps for Ausbildung Visa

Process in India
  • Consultation and explanation of the process.
  • Sign the Training and Job Agreement.
  • Pre Interview with German Job Agency.
  • Start with German Language training A1 to B1 Level.
  • Once you finish B1, Your interview process starts for a Job Contract.
  • Preparation for Resume & Motivational Letter in German.
  • Preparation of Documentation & Training for VISA Interview.
  • Submit VISA file in Germany Embassy.
  • Within in 4 weeks you get visa approval and fly to Germany.

  • Airport Pickup
  • Accommodation Arrangement by Employer (Will be low charges or No charges depends on the Employer)
  • We help with City Registration with Foreign Authorities.
